About Us

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Mumbai, Fairtex has grown from a modest textile mill into a trusted supplier of premium shirt fabrics for international markets. Our journey began decades ago with a vision to bring quality Indian textiles to the world. Today, we proudly serve customers across Indonesia, Malaysia, Greece and other regions, delivering fabrics that combine comfort, durability and style.

Our Mission

We strive to create textiles that inspire confidence and comfort. By adopting sustainable practices and leveraging cutting‑edge technology, we produce cotton, polyester and printed fabrics that meet the highest standards of quality while respecting our environment and community.

Why Choose Fairtex

  • Decades of experience in textile manufacturing and exports
  • Commitment to using high‑quality materials and meticulous craftsmanship
  • Responsive customer service and reliable global logistics
